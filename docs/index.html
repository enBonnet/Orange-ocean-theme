Why Orange Ocean?

🌞

Warm & Deep

A dark oceanic background paired with warm orange-glow accents creates a uniquely comfortable environment that reduces eye strain during extended sessions. The orange (#f47c20) provides a distinctive visual identity without being harsh.

💻

Semantic Highlighting

Every token type has a carefully chosen color: classes in orange, functions in teal, strings in cyan, keywords in purple, and parameters in warm red. Explicit scopes for 18 languages plus comprehensive general-purpose fallback coverage.

⚓

Multi-Editor Ready

Fully compatible with VS Code, Cursor, and Windsurf. The balanced palette ensures readability across all panel types, from the file explorer to the terminal, while subtle transparency layers maintain depth and focus.
